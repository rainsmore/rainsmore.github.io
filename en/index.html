Our objectives

The objective of RAINSMORE is to develop and promote innovative approach to improve rainfall monitoring worldwide, for many applications like hydrology or climate monitoring.

  • Promote the Rain Cell technique, which uses the cellular telecommunications network to estimate rainfall.
  • Conducting research and numerous capacity-building activities, with thematic workshops and many students on board
  • RAINSMORE gathers an international team, with specialists of hydrometeorology and experts in data science or deep learning,
  • We are also interested in new ideas like the use of sound recorders for rainfall detection, a technique that we tested in Amazonia.
